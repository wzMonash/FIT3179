Statewide Crime Trends in Malaysia: A Comparative Analysis

Malaysia's Crime Rate and Its Type Composition

The above visualisations show a choropleth map of the crime rates in each Malaysian states and an area chart that shows the composition of the total crimes.
Based on the choropleth map, we can see that Kuala Lumpur has the highest crime index.

The area chart shows that the common crime committed in 2016 was Motorcycle Theft.

Does population density affect the crime rate?

The above visualisations are multiple scatterplots of number of crimes versus population from the year 2016 to 2022.

We can see that in the year 2016, Kuala Lumpur has higher number of crimes for its relatively smaller population compared to Selangor. This may be due to urban poverty or the fact that Kuala Lumpur is a popular place for tourism.

However, the crime rate for Kuala Lumpur has significantly dropped compared to its initial crime rate in 2016.
As we can see from the scatterplots, population does affect the crime rate. But it's not the only potential factor.
